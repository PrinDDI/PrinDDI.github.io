About the Research

The Project Overview

Objectives

  • Identifying DDIs with empirical data: The definition of DDI as introduced by OECD is a broad and flexible one, but it is not straightforward to translate it into empirical proxies. We systematically discuss various possible definitions of DDI and make an unprecedented effort to search, select, collect, and process evidence on this phenomenon.
  • Assessing uneven impact: Drawing from the indicators and the empirical mapping developed in Objective 1, we aim to relate the present state of diffusion of DDI to the development of sectors and firms. Analyses of cross-sectoral differences will provide insights into how DDIs affect all levels of the economy.
Our Team

Our research team consists of experienced academics and researchers from various institutions, working together to achieve the project objectives.
