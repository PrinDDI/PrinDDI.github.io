Call for Papers Deadline: Nov 15, 2025

Data, Algorithms, and Innovation in Firms

A Workshop on Digital Transformation, Data Driven Innovation and Firm Dynamics

Location: Campus Economico S. Giobbe, Ca'Foscari University of Venice

Concluding workshop for the "Data Driven Innovation (DDI): Measuring its Effects on Industries, Firms, and Business Models" project. The workshop brings together scholars from economics, management, data science, and related disciplines to explore how data abundance—enabled by algorithms, AI, and digital transformation—is reshaping firms' strategy, organization, and performance.

Key Topics:

  • Data Driven Innovation & AI adoption
  • Digital transformation measurement challenges
  • Organizational change and workforce implications
  • Sustainability and firm performance

Submission: Full paper or extended abstract (800-1,500 words)
